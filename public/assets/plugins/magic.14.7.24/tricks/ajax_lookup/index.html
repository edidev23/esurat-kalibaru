AJAX Lookup (11.12.30): Magic JavaScript

Quick Jump ↵

Fetching server data when typing on the client

Usage
  • Add
    <link href="/PATH/TO/magic/tricks/ajax_lookup/ajax_lookup..css" rel="stylesheet">
    <script type="text/javascript" src="/PATH/TO/magic/tricks/ajax_lookup/ajax_lookup..js" charset="UTF-8"></script>

    below your <script type="text/javascript" src="/PATH/TO/magic/wand..js" charset="UTF-8"></script>
  • Add data-mjf="ajax_lookup|return_false" id="ELEMENT_ID" to your clickable tag.
  • Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.trick.ajax_lookup = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.
