Dropdown Menu: Magic JavaScript

Quick Jump ↵

Usage

Add <link href="/PATH/TO/magic/stylesheets/dropdown_menu..css" rel="stylesheet">

below your <script type="text/javascript" src="/PATH/TO/magic/wand..js" charset="UTF-8"></script>

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.dropdown_menu = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.

Use the example below as a guide for building your own!
