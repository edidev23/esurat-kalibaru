Google Analytics Click Tracking: Magic JavaScript

Quick Jump ↵

Usage

Add data-mjf="ga_click_track" id="ELEMENT_ID" to your clickable tag.

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.ga_click_track = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.
