Quick Jump ↵

Usage

Add data-mjf="smooth_scroll_to" data-mjf_smooth_scroll_to_id="SCROLL_TO_ID" id="ELEMENT_ID" to your <TAG_Smooth Scroll To> tag.

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.smooth_scroll_to = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.

Notes

Add data-mjf_smooth_scroll_to_top="N" to your clickable tags to scroll to N pixels above the destination.

Add data-mjf_smooth_scroll_to_left="N" to your clickable tags to scroll to N pixels to the left of the destination.
