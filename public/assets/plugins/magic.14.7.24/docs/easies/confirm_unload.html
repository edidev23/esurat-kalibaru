Confirm Click: Magic JavaScript

Quick Jump ↵

Bring up an Ok or Cancel confirmation box

Usage

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.confirm_unload = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.

Example

Reload and see!

Notes

You may also use $m.wand.confirm_unload = "Your Unload Message"; but be aware not all browsers support this.
