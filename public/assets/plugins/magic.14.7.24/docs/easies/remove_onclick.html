Remove Element (via click): Magic JavaScript

Quick Jump ↵

Deleting an element entirely from the DOM

Usage

Add data-mjf="remove_onclick|return_false" data-mjf_remove_click_id="ELEMENT_CONTENT_ID" id="ELEMENT_ID" to your clickable tag.

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.remove_onclick = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.

Example

Remove that element

<a href="#" data-mjf="remove_onclick|return_false" data-mjf_remove_click_id="remove_onclicker_cont" id="remove_onclicker">Remove that element</a>

The <p> below will be deleted after clicking the above link.

Ut at massa aliquam libero porttitor vehicula. Vivamus tristique metus velit.
