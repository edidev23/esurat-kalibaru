Usage

Add href="/PATH/TO/AUDO_FILE.EXT" data-mjf="html5_audio" data-mjf_h5a_id="ELEMENT_CONTENT_ID" data-mjf_h5a_type="mpeg(OR)ogg(OR)wav" id="ELEMENT_ID" to your <a> tag.

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.html5_audio = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.
