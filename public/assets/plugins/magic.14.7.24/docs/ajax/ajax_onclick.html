AJAX On Click: Magic JavaScript

Quick Jump ↵

Usage

Add data-mjf="ajax_click|return_false" data-mjf_ajax_url="/PATH/TO/SERVER/SCRIPT/" data-mjf_ajax_update_id="ELEMENT_ID" id="ELEMENT_ID" to your clickable tag.

Add <script type="text/javascript">$m.wand.ajax_click = true;</script> inside your <body> tag.
